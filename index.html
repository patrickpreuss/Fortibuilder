Fortibuilder

Converter that will eventually be released to help Administrators of Fortigate Firewalls.

Currently will convert Cisco ASA and portions of checkpoint configuration files, along with a generic parser to make objects from any .csv file.

Still need to upload a bunch of fixes completed earlier from a local repo. I would not suggest using it until then unless you know what you're doing.
